Architecutre

Except for Effiel Tower, there are many beautiful architectures in Paris, one of the most famous buildings in the world is the Louvre Museum. It is the world's second largest art museum and a historic monument in Pairs. It is best known for being the home of the "Mona Lisa".

Since the opening of Agusut 10th, 1793 with an exhibition of 537 paintings, it has expanded greatly under Napoleon's ruling. Now the Louvre Museum contains more than 380,00 objects and siplays 35,000 works of art with 60,600 square metres, the Louvre has been the undisputed "most popular museum" in the world. (Wikipedia)
